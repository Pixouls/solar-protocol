Natural Intelligence

Solar Protocol reconfigures internet protocols using natural rather than artificial intelligence. When visiting most large internet platforms today, decisions about what content you see are automated with a type of artificial intelligence called machine learning. Machine learning systems are trained on massive datasets collected via online surveillance. They produce logic from patterns within their data and use this logic to automate decisions. Most large web platforms collect data from every click, view and scroll and then use this data to automate decisions about what content to show their users. In contrast to this, on the Solar Protocol platform, decisions about what content is served is automated with a logic produced by available sunshine and energy. The network operates with a natural intelligence that emerges from the intermittent dynamics of our shared environment.

Our current imagination for the internet and computing at large, has been shaped by the energy systems that power it. Cheap, fossil fuel energy has produced a culture that celebrates speed and ever larger, heavier media. These energy systems have also made current forms of artificial intelligence possible. As the author Timothy Mitchell observes, different energy regimes create different political possibilities and limitations [1]. In Solar Protocol, by changing the way a computer network is powered, we aim to expand the imaginative potential for what the internet could be, as well as reshaping expectations of it. Other worlds are possible.2

Working with small solar powered servers makes it advantageous to reduce the size of the website that you want to serve as much as possible. This is why we have omitted energy-intensive tracking technologies which forecloses the possibility of online surveillance and the online business models that go with it.[3] Solar power therefore encourages a lighter internet that is customized to collective environmental conditions rather than individual user behavior.

By reconfiguring internet protocols with the logic of the sun, Solar Protocol seeks to create a space for working with natural intelligence and exploring it might change our relationship to the internet for the better.
